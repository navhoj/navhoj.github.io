Greetings!

Welcome to my website 🙂

I’ve always been fascinated by human behaviour; my ultimate enigma is why do people behave as they do. My other source of enthusiasm is figures and stories they could tell. Studying statistics as my major at the undergraduate level, this fondness which I had, turned into affection, and I began to master the statistical inference with various software, rigorously. I have had the privilege to be a freelance statistical consultant in many master’s theses and Ph.D. dissertations, mostly in the fields of economics, management and psychology. Years of practice as a statistical consultant have given me analytical skills as well as the expertise to research in quantitative and qualitative areas. During my Master's program I've encountered the illuminating field of behavioural sciences. In particular, studying behavioural economics captivated me, because it comprises knowledge to illuminate the way in searching for answers to my questions. After that, I became more acquainted with machine learning by viewing its applications and grew more interested in artificial intelligence and its ability to help humans in various matters, especially decision-making and predicting behaviour.

If you want to reach me, do not hesitate to contact me by sending an email or message, or write me something lovely that makes my day. You are more than welcome to ask me about statistical methods in the research context, and I am open to collaborations in that field.

Thank you kindly.🌼
